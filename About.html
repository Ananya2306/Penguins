Amazing Facts About Penguins.........................

Penguin Awareness Day is on January 20th, and there are tons of surprising facts to learn about penguins.

  • There are at least 17 species of penguins.
  • The smallest type of penguin is only around 10 inches tall.
  • There was once a "mega" penguin that stood 6.5 feet tall and weighed more than 250 pounds.
  • Penguins can't fly, but they CAN become airborne. Some can leap as high as nine feet!
  • Penguins' black and white coloring is the perfect camouflage for swimming in the ocean.
  • Penguins are carnivores.
  • Some penguins can reach speeds of 22 miles an hour.
  • The deepest dive ever made by a penguin was more than 6,000 feet underwater.
  • Less than 4,000 of the rarest kind of penguin exists today.
  • Penguins may huddle together for several reasons.
